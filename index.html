What we're making

A short film to launch the collection

The Ascent Collection is a set of two gold-plated collector coins and a heritage box, honouring the Pilots of the Caribbean and the Tuskegee Airmen. We're making a short film to launch it, so people can see the collection before it goes on sale.

This page is a private preview of what we've built so far. We'll keep it up to date as the work moves on.

The film

Three lengths, one story

The main film is a 90-second trailer, with shorter 60- and 30-second versions for social media. It opens by showing the box being opened, so most of the work right now is on the box itself and the pieces that go inside it.

The presentation box

Built to film from any angle

We rebuilt the navy box as a 3-D model, so it can be lit and filmed either closed or open. The gold "Together We Triumph" lettering on the lid stays crisp in every shot.

The booklet cover

Aircraft to Supercar

The cover of the booklet, rebuilt piece by piece: the Rolls-Royce badge, a red-tailed P-51 Mustang and a Spitfire, the Bentley Continental GT, and the Bentley / Jack Barclay lettering.

The coin cases

One for each group

Two cases, matched as a pair: navy for the Pilots of the Caribbean, crimson for the Tuskegee Airmen. We're finishing the logo placement on the lids.

How it's coming together

Where things stand

Most of it is built

The box is done and most of the pieces are made. A few still need finishing, and then everything goes into the box and filming begins. There's a full breakdown on the Status page.

Two things to keep on file. Some of the remaining pieces need the real product photos before we can rebuild them. And the Rolls-Royce, Bentley and King Charles images need their usage rights kept on record for the launch.
