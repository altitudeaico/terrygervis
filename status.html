The Deliverable

Where the project stands

The headline deliverable is the cinematic launch film — a 90-second hero cut with 60 and 30-second cut-downs for the pre-sale. The build effort right now is on the museum box and its ten heritage pieces, which feed the film's signature "box opens" reveal and the value-stack sequence. The container tier is complete; most of the ten pieces are built, with a final set still to produce before the box interior is assembled and filming begins.
